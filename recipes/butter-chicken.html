Butter Chicken

Fancy a healthy version of your favorite Friday night chicken curry?

The chicken can be marinaded the day before so you can get ahead on your prep

Ingredients needed

For the marinade
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1-2 tsp hot chilli powder
  • 200g natural yogurt
For the curry
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
